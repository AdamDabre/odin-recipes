Beef and Broccoli

Stats

Course: Main

Serves: 4 People

Time: 33 mins

Ingreidents
  • Half a kilo of flank steak,cut into 1/4-inch thick strips
  • 1 pound broccoli florets (about 1 1/2 quarts)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce (divided)
  • 1/4 cup oyster sauce (divided)
  • 1 diced opnion
  • 1 diced garlic
  • 1 diced ginger
  • 2 teaspoons corn starch
  • 1/3 cup low-sodium homemade or store-bought chicken stock

Method
  • In an oiled wok raised to meduim heat,fry the stake unitll its cooked all the way through, then remove from pan into a plate and put it aside
  • Add some more oil , thena dd the ginger and garlic, cook untill soften
  • Add all liquid ingreidents to that bowl, mix and stir well
  • Add the broccoli
  • In a differnt bowl add the cornstach and some water together, stitl until there are no more cunks then add to the wok and raise till a boil
  • Then add the cooked beef into the mix and mix well
  • Serve with/over some rice
