Beef and Broccoli

Course: Main

Serves: 4 People

Time: 33 mins

Ingreidents
  • Half a kilo of flank steak,cut into 1/4-inch thick strips
  • 1 pound broccoli florets (about 1 1/2 quarts)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce (divided)
  • 1/4 cup oyster sauce (divided)
  • 1/4 cup shaoxing wine or dry sherry (divided)
  • 2 teaspoons corn starch
  • 1/3 cup low-sodium homemade or store-bought chicken stock

Method
  • In bowl,whisk together the cornstach with some water, add the beef into that bowl.
