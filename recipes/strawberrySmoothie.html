Strawberry Smoothie

Course: Drink/snack

Serves: 4 People

Time: 5 Mins

Ingreidents
  1. 3 cups Frozon Strawberry
  2. 1 and 1/2 cups milk (any type)
  3. 2 teaspoons of sugar

Ingreidents
  1. Add the strawberreis,milk and sugars into a blender
  2. Close the blender
  3. Blend untill smooth
  4. Serve in a cup
